Bayou Billy > Characters
Billy West - Main Character. Wears a cowboy hat. Trying to save his love, Anabelle, from evil Gordon. Billy specializes in Kung Fu, but is also very good with other weapons such as whips and guns.
Annabelle Lane - Billy's lover. Kidnapped by Gordon. Wears a skimpy tanktop and incredibly short shorts. Is waiting for Billy to come and save her.
Godfather Gordon - "The Gangster King of Bourbon Street." Chubby guy who wears a nice blue suit along with a hat. Though he may not look it, the man is quite skilled in fighting and especially dodging. He kidnaps Annebelle Lane for no given reason.
Rocky and Rocco - "Heirs to Gordon's Underworld Throne." The final bosses. Strong fighters capable of crushing any man when working together. When you are fighting them, one of them grabs you while the other punches the living daylights out of you. The trick to defeating them is hit and run.

Bayou Billy > Walkthrough > Stage 1
STAGE 1
Encounter 1: Bad Guy A (Blue)
- Jump Kick this guy to death.

Encounter 2: Bad Guy A, Bad Guy B (Red)
- The new enemy is faster and harder. Same Jump Kicking strategy.

Encounter 3: Bad Guy A, Bad Guy A, Bad Guy B
- Jump Kick around or else it's going to hurt.

Encounter 4: Crocodiles, Birds
- Jump Kick through the water to go faster. Watch out for crocs and birds.

Encounter 5: Fatman, Fatman, Fatman
- Two have huge stones, the other one has a stick. Get the stick, it'll help.

Encounter 6: Bad Guy B
- This guy has a knife. Dodge the knife and pound him with your stick. You'll lose your stick if you pick up the knife, so don't.

Encounter 7: Crocodiles
- Kill the crocs, you're forced to this time. Use your stick, nothing else.

Encounter 8: Scuba Guy A, Scuba Guy A, Scuba Guy B
- The one in white has a bullet-proof vest. The guys in grey have guns. You get 6 bullets per gun. Go after the one in white first, cause he's annoying.

Encounter 9: Punk, Scuba Guy A, Scuba Guy A
- Use your gun for lots of fun.

Encounter 10: Punk, Scuba Guy B
- Shoot the Punk and punch the Scuba Guy til his vest falls of. Then shoot the Scuba Guy once.

Encounter 11: Crocodiles
- Avoid the crocs and jump kick til the end of the stage.
